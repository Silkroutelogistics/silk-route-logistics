Khotan, Our TMS

Khotan is the SRL operational stack. Built in house, owned end to end. Every load, every tender, every document, every settlement runs through one chain. Records stay on SRL infrastructure, accessible when you ask.

For Shippers

From quote to delivery, one operational record. From dispute to resolution, one audit trail.

  • Operational record, intact. Quote, tender, document, charge, dispute on one chain. Searchable on day one, exportable on day one thousand.
  • Your brand carries the trip. Tracking links forward to your end customer on the SRL domain. Your brand on the operational artifact, not a broker stamp.
  • Disputes resolve in the system that created them. Every adjustment timestamped, every counter on the record.
  • One direct line. A named Account Executive on every load. The person who knows your freight is the person who answers the phone.

For Carriers

From tender to settlement, one workflow. From compliance to capacity, one scorecard.

  • Your scorecard, transparent. Seven factors, weights published, your number live in the portal. The same data the dispatch desk sees.
  • Quick Pay in your control. Tier aware fees published, settlement direct to you. The factor stays out of the relationship.
  • POD to invoice in one motion. Upload at the dock, invoice fires. Quick Pay clears on your schedule.
  • Dispatch with a name. Direct line to a trained broker. Tier progression you can see and earn.

AI is a lever, not a replacement. Khotan holds the data of record. Compass Engine vets every carrier. Marco Polo AI answers questions around the clock. Behind all three, trained Account Executives are on duty Monday through Friday 7am to 7pm Eastern, with an after hours emergency line on every active load.
