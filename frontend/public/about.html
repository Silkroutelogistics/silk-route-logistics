Our Heritage

Our Story

Silk Route Logistics was founded with a singular vision: to bring transparency and technology to an industry that has traditionally operated in the dark. In freight brokerage, information asymmetry has long been the norm — shippers don't know where their freight is, carriers don't know when they'll get paid, and everyone is left guessing. We set out to change that.

Our name pays homage to the ancient Silk Road — the world's first logistics network that connected civilizations, facilitated trade, and built bridges between cultures spanning thousands of miles. Just as those historic routes thrived on trust and mutual benefit, SRL is built on the principle that lasting success comes from creating value for every partner in the supply chain.

Today, headquartered in Kalamazoo, Michigan — strategically positioned in the heart of the Midwest freight corridor — Silk Route Logistics connects shippers and carriers across the continent with the same spirit of partnership and reliability. We leverage cutting-edge technology to optimize every shipment, providing real-time visibility, data-driven pricing, and proactive communication that our partners can depend on.
