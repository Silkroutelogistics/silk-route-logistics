How updates reach you

From the truck to your screen, in under a minute.

Every status you see was sourced from a real check-call: a driver phone-in, an EDI 214 message, an ELD GPS ping, a carrier-portal entry, or a geofence trigger. SRL operations logs it, tags the source, recomputes ETA, and publishes it here — usually within 60 seconds of the source event.

  • Phone to operations — driver calls (269) 220-6760; AE logs on the load
  • EDI 214 — carrier's TMS sends a status code; auto-mapped to load state
  • ELD GPS — every-15-minute location ping from the truck's ELD device
  • Carrier portal — driver or dispatcher submits status from the mobile portal
  • Geofence — entering / leaving origin or destination fires automatically

Every update on your tracker shows the source it came from and the timestamp. Tracking is no-login — anyone with the BOL or reference number can see it. For signed PODs, multi-load views, and exports, the shipper portal (sign in above) is the authenticated home.
