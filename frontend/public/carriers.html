Compass Score, Published and Weighted

Mathematical scoring. No black box.

Click to learn more

How it works

  • Every load you run is scored on seven measurable factors with published weights: pickup 20%, delivery 20%, tracking 15%, claims 15%, communication 10%, documents 10%, acceptance 10%.
  • Compass Engine recalculates your composite score after each delivery and check-call.
  • Your score and the inputs driving it are visible in the carrier portal anytime you log in.
  • Tier advancement is mathematical. No broker opinion, no black box.

Itemized Quotes, No Post-Booking Surprises

Rate confirmation equals settlement total.

Click to learn more

How it works

  • Every rate confirmation lists line-haul, fuel surcharge, and accessorials as separate line items.
  • The total printed on the rate confirmation is the total you see on settlement. No mystery deductions.
  • Post-dispatch accessorials (detention, lumper, stop-off) are added transparently with documentation.
  • Every adjustment shows the math. Nothing subtracted silently between booking and pay.

Quick Pay Without a Factoring Contract

Per-load. Optional. No contract.

Click to learn more

How it works

  • Quick Pay is per-load and optional. Elect it on the invoices that need cash flow, skip it on the ones that don't.
  • No 12–24 month factoring contract. No termination fees. No letter of release on most invoices.
  • Published tier fees: Silver 3%, Gold 2%, Platinum 1% on 7-day pay. Same-day adds a universal 2%.
  • Standard pay is always free: Silver Net-30, Gold Net-21, Platinum Net-14.
