Commitments

What SRL commits to, on every load

Three operating principles, tested on every rate confirmation and every settlement. Not marketing language — enforceable terms.

Commitment 1

Compass Score, Published and Weighted

Every carrier is scored on seven measurable factors with published weights: on-time pickup (20%), on-time delivery (20%), GPS compliance (15%), claims ratio (15%), communication (10%), document timeliness (10%), acceptance rate (10%). Tier advancement is mathematical, not opinion. Your score and the inputs driving it are visible in the carrier portal at every recalculation — no broker scoring black box.

Commitment 2

Itemized Quotes, No Post-Booking Surprises

Every rate confirmation lists line-haul, fuel surcharge, and accessorials as separate line items. The total on the rate confirmation is the total on settlement — no mystery deductions between booking and pay. If an accessorial applies post-dispatch (detention, lumper, stop-off), it is added transparently with documentation, not subtracted silently.

Commitment 3

Quick Pay Without a Factoring Contract

SRL's Quick Pay is per-load and optional. No 12–24 month factoring contract. No termination fees. No letter of release required on most invoices (only if you have already factored a specific load with a third party). Use Quick Pay when cash flow matters. Skip it when it doesn't. Standard pay is always free: Silver Net-30, Gold Net-21, Platinum Net-14.
