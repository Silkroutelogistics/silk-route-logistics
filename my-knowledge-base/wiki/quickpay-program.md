SRL's QuickPay program directly addresses the #1 carrier survival issue: cash flow. With $70K initial capital, SRL can support ~35 simultaneous loads with payment recycling every 30 days. The program saves carriers $3,600-$4,500/year vs traditional factoring.

## Fee Structure (Tiered via [[srcpp-program]])

| Tier | Fee | Payment Speed | Carrier Segment |
|------|-----|---------------|-----------------|
| GUEST/BRONZE | 3% | Next-day | New carriers |
| SILVER | 2.5% | 2-day | Regular performers |
| GOLD | 2% | 3-day | Strong performers |
| PLATINUM | 1.5% | 3-day | Top performers |

## Market Context
- $200.76B freight factoring market (projected $437.4B by 2035)
- 70%+ of small carriers use factoring at 4-5.5% effective rate
- Industry QP standard: 2% for 2-3 day payment (CHR 2%, TQL 1.5-2%, Uber Freight 0-2%)
- 30-day payment delay costs carriers $13,000-$48,000/year in real costs

## Recommended Platform: Denim Wallet
- 100% fee retention for broker (no cut of QP fees)
- Self-funded QP model
- API available for integration
- Connects to AscendTMS, Tai, QuickBooks

## Four Critical Gaps (from [[carrier-pain-points]])
1. **No fuel card** — factoring companies bundle 5-15 cents/gallon discounts
2. **No fuel advance at pickup** — 50-80% advance is factoring's #1 benefit
3. **No same-day payment tier** — factoring offers instant; SRL fastest is next-day
4. **No detention pay guarantee** — $11.5B industry problem
