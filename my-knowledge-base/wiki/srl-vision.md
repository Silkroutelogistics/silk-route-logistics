SRL's strategic north star: be THE most user-friendly freight platform for carriers in the industry. Inspired by Bison Transport's organizational structure (Wasih's prior employer), SRL prioritizes carrier self-service, transparency, and performance-based rewards.

## Core Thesis
Carriers are the supply side of freight. Treat them as partners, not interchangeable trucks. If carriers prefer hauling SRL loads, SRL wins — shippers follow capacity.

## Carrier-First Principles
1. **Transparent everything** — rates, payment status, compliance scores visible in portal
2. **Performance rewards** — [[srcpp-program]] tiers unlock better terms (not just for SRL's benefit)
3. **Self-service** — load selection, analytics, payment tracking, compliance dashboard without calling a broker
4. **Speed-to-pay** — [[quickpay-program]] as core differentiator vs factoring
5. **No double-brokering** — explicit commitment, carriers trust their load stays on their truck

## Bison Transport Model
Organizational structure modeled on:
- Regional logistics teams with dedicated analysts
- Customer service tiers (Assistant → Rep → AE Assistant → Sales Executive)
- In-house track & trace, compliance (RMIS integration), fuel optimization
- Credit review, AR/AP, CFO-level financial reporting

## Long-Term Vision
- API integration with major brokerages (CHR, Echo, Coyote, XPO, TQL, Uber Freight)
- Carriers see ALL loads/payments across the industry in one SRL portal
- SRL becomes the carrier's operating system, not just another broker
